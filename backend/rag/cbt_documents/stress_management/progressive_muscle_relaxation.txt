Title: Progressive Muscle Relaxation
Category: Stress Management

Overview:
Progressive muscle relaxation involves tensing and releasing muscle groups to reduce physical stress.

Steps:
1. Tense a muscle group for 5 seconds.
2. Release for 10 seconds.
3. Move systematically from feet to head.

Why It Works:
Releasing muscle tension signals safety to the nervous system.

Therapeutic Goal:
To decrease physical stress symptoms.
