Title: Stress Reduction Through Time Structuring
Category: Stress Management

Overview:
Structured scheduling reduces overwhelm and increases perceived control.

Steps:
1. List tasks.
2. Prioritize top three.
3. Allocate realistic time blocks.
4. Include rest breaks.

Why It Works:
Unstructured workload increases anxiety. Planning reduces uncertainty.

Therapeutic Goal:
To improve stress coping capacity.
