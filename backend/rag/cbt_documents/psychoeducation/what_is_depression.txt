Title: Understanding Depression
Category: Psychoeducation

Overview:
Depression is a mood condition characterized by persistent sadness, loss of interest, and reduced energy.

Common Symptoms:
- Low mood
- Fatigue
- Sleep disturbances
- Reduced motivation
- Feelings of hopelessness

When to Seek Help:
If symptoms persist for more than two weeks or interfere with daily life.

Therapeutic Goal:
To increase awareness and reduce stigma.
