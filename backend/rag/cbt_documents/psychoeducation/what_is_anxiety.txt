Title: Understanding Anxiety
Category: Psychoeducation

Overview:
Anxiety is a natural response to perceived threat. It prepares the body to respond quickly.

Common Symptoms:
- Rapid heartbeat
- Sweating
- Racing thoughts
- Muscle tension

When Problematic:
Anxiety becomes concerning when it interferes with daily functioning or persists without clear triggers.

Therapeutic Goal:
To normalize anxiety while identifying when support is needed.
