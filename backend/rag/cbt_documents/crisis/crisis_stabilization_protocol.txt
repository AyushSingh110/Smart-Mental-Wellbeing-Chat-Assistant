Title: Immediate Crisis Stabilization Protocol
Category: Crisis Intervention

Overview:
This protocol is for individuals experiencing severe emotional distress or suicidal thoughts.

Immediate Steps:
1. Pause and take slow breaths.
2. Remove access to harmful objects.
3. Contact a trusted person immediately.
4. Reach out to local emergency services or suicide helplines.

Important:
This assistant cannot replace professional or emergency care.

Therapeutic Goal:
To ensure immediate safety and stabilization.
