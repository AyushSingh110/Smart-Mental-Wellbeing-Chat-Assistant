Title: Small Steps Activation Method
Category: Behavioral Activation

Overview:
The small steps method focuses on reducing overwhelming tasks into manageable components.

Steps:
1. Define a large task.
2. Break it into the smallest possible step.
3. Complete only that step.
4. Gradually increase effort.

Why It Works:
Depression reduces energy and motivation. Small achievable goals rebuild momentum.

Therapeutic Goal:
To restore confidence and productivity gradually.
