Title: Behavioral Activation Through Scheduling
Category: Behavioral Activation

Overview:
Behavioral activation targets depression by increasing engagement in meaningful activities.

Steps:
1. Identify one small enjoyable activity.
2. Schedule it at a specific time.
3. Complete the activity regardless of motivation level.
4. Track mood before and after.

Why It Works:
Action often precedes motivation. Increasing activity can improve mood.

Therapeutic Goal:
To break cycles of avoidance and inactivity.
