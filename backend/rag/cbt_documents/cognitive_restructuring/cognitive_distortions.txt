Title: Common Cognitive Distortions
Category: Cognitive Restructuring

Overview:
Cognitive distortions are systematic patterns of biased thinking that contribute to emotional distress.

Examples:
- All-or-nothing thinking
- Catastrophizing
- Overgeneralization
- Mind reading
- Emotional reasoning
- Personalization

Why It Matters:
Recognizing distorted thinking is the first step toward modifying it.

Therapeutic Goal:
To increase awareness of inaccurate thought patterns.
